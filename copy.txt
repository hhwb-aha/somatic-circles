## 14. FAQ

**Section Title:**
Frequently Asked Questions

**Who can benefit from Somatic Circles?**
Anyone carrying chronic stress, tension, physical discomfort, or emotional patterns that haven't shifted through conventional approaches. No prior experience with somatic work is required.

**Do I need any prior experience with TRE?**
No. Somatic Circles is designed to take you from zero to confident practitioner over 6 weeks. You'll learn everything you need from the ground up.

**Is this therapy?**
No. Somatic Circles is a coaching and education program. TRE is a self-regulation practice, not a clinical treatment. If you are currently under psychiatric care, please consult your provider before joining.

**How long are the sessions?**
Each session is 90 minutes, once a week for 6 weeks.

**What's the difference between online and in-person?**
The content and experience are the same. In-person participants additionally receive the Somatic Circles digital self-paced workshop as a bonus. In-person sessions are held in Bangalore.

**How many people are in each group?**
6 people maximum. This is intentional — the small container is part of what makes the work possible.

**What if I miss a session?**
Sessions are not recorded, as the live group dynamic is central to the experience. If you miss a session, your facilitator will send you a summary of what was covered and you can continue with the group the following week.

**When does the next cohort start?**
Dates will be announced once spots are confirmed. Early bird pricing is available now — securing your spot locks in your rate regardless of the start date.

**What equipment or space do I need?**
For online participants, a quiet space with enough room to lie down on a mat. A yoga mat is ideal but not required. A stable internet connection for video calls.
