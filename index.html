Frequently Asked Questions

Who can benefit from Somatic Circles™?

Anyone who has experienced stress, tension, or trauma can benefit from TRE™. It's especially helpful for individuals dealing with chronic stress, anxiety, physical tension, or past trauma. Individuals also report being far calmer and present in their relationships. TRE™ is also widely used across the globe by healthcare professionals, first responders (emergency workers), athletes, and individuals in high-stress environments.

Do I need any prior experience with any therapy or TRE™?

Not at all. Somatic Circles™ is designed to take you from zero to confident practitioner over 6 weeks. You'll learn everything you need from the ground up and you'll have plenty of opportunity to practice till you are confident.

Is this trauma therapy?

Somatic Circles™ is a coaching and educational program, and TRE™ is a body-based self-regulation practice. TRE™ can support trauma release processes in the body, but it should not be used as a substitute for psychotherapy, medical care, or trauma treatment. It can complement these modalities very nicely. If you are currently under psychiatric care, please consult your provider before joining.

Is this considered medical treatment?

No. TRE™ is not positioned as a medical solution yet. However it is worth mentioning that many practitioners do report evidence of chronic tension and pain reducing/disappearing over time as their nervous system works to heal the body from within.

Can I do this if I am in therapy?

Yes, absolutely. TRE™ can complement talk therapy by addressing physiological stress patterns alongside cognitive work.

Is TRE™ safe?

Yes, TRE™ is safe for most people when practiced responsibly. It's a self-guided process, and individuals can stop or modify the exercises if they feel uncomfortable. However, those with severe trauma or medical conditions should consult us before signing up — write to us at coach (at) holistichealingwithb (dot) com or WhatsApp us at +91-8088015825.

How long are the sessions?

Each session is 90 minutes, once a week, for 6 weeks.

How many people are in each group?

6 people maximum. The small container is part of what makes the work effective and possible.

What if I miss a session?

The live group dynamic is central to the experience and so recordings of sessions are not provided. If you need to miss a session, please get in touch with your facilitator. We advise not missing any sessions for your own benefit and smooth learning process.

How long do I need to practice for each day?

TRE™ does not need to be a daily practice. Initially, it's enough to do it 1–2x per week. The exercises may take 15–20 mins, and then tremoring for about 10 mins.

As you get comfortable with the process and your body can access the tremor mechanism easily, you might find you just tremor for a couple minutes on a particular day and you're good to go. You can tremor as often as you feel your body needs.

The body is the best guide for how often to practice, and how long your practice should be on a given day.

How long do I need to practice TRE™ to see changes?

TRE™ can be a life-long practice. :) Change happens through regular practice of TRE™, not how intense a practice session is.

Some people begin noticing major shifts after just 4 weeks, many around 8 weeks. For others, it may take longer, depending on their body.

The nervous system unlearns and relearns safety gradually, so it's really important to be patient, not judge your body, and just listen to what it needs. :)

What equipment or space do I need?

For online participants, a mobile or laptop to join the session from a quiet space. A yoga mat is ideal but not required. A stable internet connection will be required as you will need to have your video on, so that your facilitator can see you and give you feedback.

Will I lose control while tremoring?

The tremor response is always fully within your control, and can be stopped at any time you feel uncomfortable. You will be guided to listen to your body and build capacity gradually.

What if I am skeptical of somatic release?

It's natural to feel skeptical about trying something new. TRE™ is backed by research and has helped hundreds of thousands of people worldwide reduce stress and tension. The process is gentle, self-guided, and doesn't require belief to work. Your body's natural tremoring mechanism simply activates as a physical response.

You can start slowly, observe how your body reacts, and stop at any time if you feel uncomfortable. Many individuals who were initially skeptical have found it to be a powerful and transformative practice.
